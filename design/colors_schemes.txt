##############
KLEURSCHEMA'S
##############

All kleurenschema's hebben allen drie kleurenkanalen: rood, groen en blauw.

- # hexadecimaal of alfanumeriek
- RGB() en RGBA()

ZWART

#ffffff
rgb(000, 000, 000)

WIT

rgb(255, 255, 255)
#000000

RGBA heeft een extra kanaal alpha voor transparantie
0 is maximale transparantie / 1 is de normale weergave.

rgba(155, 200, 110, .5)

####################
PICKING COLOR SCHEME
####################

To find colors that blend well together use online tools such as UI COLOR PICKER or PALLETON.

Some people have done the hard work for us and categorized various colors that go well with black, gray, white, etc. backgrounds: Categorized Color* Charts.

For common color color codes see HTMLCOLORCODES.

########
CONTRAST
########

Especially when it comes to buttons and text on a colored background, check the contrast ratios and see if they meet the standards.

During the design process in AdobeXd you can use the plugin STARK.
